journalFishingTutorialV2,"In order to fish, you will need a fishing rod. Once you've got a rod, find a body of water to stand in. You must be atleast 5% wet, or about ankle deep, in order for fishing to work. You may also craft and place fishing chairs on your docks or favorite locations to fish from. Standing on one of these will allow fishing.\n\nTo begin fishing, cast your line with left click. You'll have a chance for a fish to bite over time. This chance can be modified via perks from living off the land, or from using bait. When you get a bite, your screen will begin to shake and you will hear splashing. Quickly reel in by tapping F (or the button to turn on and off lights). Get 100 or more points and you'll get your catch.\n\nDifferent fish can be caught in different biomes, as well as different times (day or night), so be sure to explore and see what you can catch!\n\nBait can be applied to your rod to increase the chance a fish will bite, which can make it faster to get fish for food. Other bait can be caught using a bugnet. Craft and equip one, then crouch and you'll be able to hear bugs around you.
